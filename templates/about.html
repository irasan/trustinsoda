Who we are

Here at Upside Down Recruiters we aim to make the workplace a more welcoming and accessible environment. People with disabilities are more likely to be unemployed and face more difficulty than the average person when trying to enter the workforce. We hope to work with companies and other recuitment agencies to change this. Our team is currently focussing on working with people with Down Syndrome to try erase the misconception that they are unable to work. Our site has an easy to follow sign up process for those looking for work. When the profile is complete, just sit back and relax and let the employers come to you!

Who you are

As a user of our website you're either a person with Down Syndrome looking for help finding a job, or you are an employer looking to hire someone. As a job seeker all you need to do is follow this link to sign up, enter the details via the form or chatbot and this allows potential employers to find you. It's that simple! As an employer you need to register here and you'll be able to view profiles of people looking to work.
